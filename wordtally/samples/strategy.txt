A good plan is the heart of any strategy. When you plan well, you win
before the battle begins. The best plan looks at the ground, the weather,
and the people who must carry it out. A plan that ignores the ground is a
plan that fails. Plan for what you can see, and plan for what you cannot.
The wise leader keeps the plan simple, and keeps the plan flexible, because
the ground always shifts once the work begins.
